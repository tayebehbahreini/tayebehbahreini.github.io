About me

I am a postdoctoral research scientist in IBM T.J. Watson Research Center under mentorship of Dr. Asser Tantawi . I recieved the PhD degree from Department of Computer Science at Wayne State University under supervision of Dr. Daniel Grosu in 2021. I received the M.Sc. in computer engineering from Shahed University, Iran, in 2014, and the B.Sc. degree in computer science from University of Isfahan, Iran, in 2010. My main research interests are distributed systems, cloud computing, edge computing, parallel computing, approximation algorithms, and electric vehicles. I am a member of ACM, IEEE, and INFORMS.
